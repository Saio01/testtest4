Siargao

Siargao is an island of nine municipalities in the province of Surigao del Norte. Known as the “Surfing Capital of the Philippines”, Siargao is mainly responsible for introducing surfing to the country.


FAVE PLACES IN SIARGAO

  • Tayangban Cave Pool : Swimming, climbing & cliff jumping in & around a partially submerged grotto surrounded by mangroves.

  • Alegria Beach: Alegria Beach is the most beautiful beach. It is a stunning white sand beach with the most amazing turquoise and transparent water that is located in the island's most northern region. The beach isn't crowded.

  • Cloud 9 Surfing Area : One of the best-known surfing spots in Siargao is Cloud 9, to which the town owes its surfing legacy. Cloud 9 is world-famous for its thick, hollow tubes and fast-barreling right-hand waves that break over a reef.
